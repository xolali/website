## Support Process

**Eyebrow:** How support works

**Headline:**
## Getting help is simple.

**Step 1 — Search the Help Center**
Start with our [Help Center](/help). It has step-by-step setup guides, troubleshooting articles, and answers to the most common questions about AfroVPN, your account, and billing. Most issues are solved here in minutes.

**Step 2 — Submit a request**
Can't find your answer? Submit a support request by email or through the contact form. Tell us what's happening and what you've already tried — the more detail you give us, the faster we can help.

**Step 3 — We investigate and respond**
A member of our support team reviews your request and responds within 24 hours, usually sooner. For account and billing matters, we may verify your identity to keep your account secure.

**Step 4 — We resolve and follow up**
We'll work with you until your issue is fully resolved. Once it is, we'll confirm with you and close the ticket — and we're always one message away if anything else comes up.

## Ticket Workflow

**Eyebrow:** What happens after you contact us

**Headline:**
## Every request, tracked from start to finish.

When you submit a support request, here's exactly what happens:

**1. Received & acknowledged**
You'll get an automatic confirmation with a ticket reference number. Keep it — it lets you track your request and helps us find your history quickly.

**2. Triaged & prioritized**
We categorize your request (getting started, account, billing, connection, or privacy) and prioritize it. Urgent issues — like a billing error or an account you can't access — move to the front of the queue.

**3. Assigned to a specialist**
Your ticket is routed to a support team member with the right expertise. For security-sensitive or billing matters, we may ask you to verify your identity before we make any changes.

**4. Investigated & answered**
We work your issue and respond with a clear solution or the next step. If we need more information, we'll ask specific questions to keep things moving.

**5. Resolved & confirmed**
Once your issue is solved, we confirm with you before closing the ticket. If it isn't fully resolved, the ticket stays open and we keep working.

**6. Closed — but never final**
A closed ticket can always be reopened. If the same issue returns or you have a follow-up, just reply to the thread and we'll pick up right where we left off.

**Ticket status reference:**
- **Open** — received, awaiting our first response
- **In progress** — a specialist is actively working on it
- **Awaiting your reply** — we need information from you to continue
- **Resolved** — solved and confirmed with you
- **Closed** — complete (and reopenable anytime)

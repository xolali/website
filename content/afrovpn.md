## Product Overview

**Eyebrow:** What is AfroVPN

**Headline:**
## A private tunnel between you and the open internet.

**Body:**
Every time you go online, your data passes through networks you don't control — your internet provider, public Wi-Fi, the websites you visit. They can see where you go, slow you down, or block you entirely.

AfroVPN changes that. It creates an encrypted tunnel between your device and the internet, so your connection is private, your data is protected, and the open web is fully within reach. No tracking. No throttling. No artificial walls.

We built AfroVPN for the realities of African connectivity — mobile-first, efficient on limited bandwidth, and fast where it counts — and connected it to a global network so you're never cut off from the rest of the world.

## Features

**Eyebrow:** Features

**Headline:**
## Everything you need to take back your connection.

**Feature 1 — Bank-grade encryption**
Your traffic is secured with AES-256 encryption — the same standard trusted by banks and governments. Anyone intercepting your connection sees nothing but noise.

**Feature 2 — Strict no-logs policy**
We don't monitor, record, or store what you do online. Your browsing history is yours alone — we couldn't hand it over even if we were asked, because we never keep it.

**Feature 3 — Global server network**
Connect through servers in 40+ countries. Reach content and services from anywhere, with optimized routing that keeps speeds high and latency low.

**Feature 4 — Built for African networks**
Optimized for mobile data, intermittent connectivity, and the conditions of real African networks — so it works where other VPNs struggle.

**Feature 5 — All your devices, one account**
Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Protect multiple devices on a single subscription and switch between them seamlessly.

**Feature 6 — Kill switch protection**
If your VPN connection ever drops, the kill switch instantly blocks your traffic — so your real IP and data are never exposed, not even for a second.

**Feature 7 — One-tap connection**
Open the app, tap once, and you're protected. No configuration, no complexity. Security that gets out of your way.

**Feature 8 — No bandwidth limits**
Stream, download, browse, and work without caps. Your connection, unrestricted.

## Security Section

**Eyebrow:** Security & privacy

**Headline:**
## Security isn't a feature of AfroVPN. It's the whole point.

**Body:**
We built AfroVPN so that the safe choice is the only choice. Here's exactly how we protect you — and what we will never do.

**How we protect you:**
- **AES-256 encryption** secures every connection end to end.
- **Secure VPN protocols** (including WireGuard® and OpenVPN) balance speed and security.
- **Automatic kill switch** prevents any data leaking if the connection drops.
- **DNS leak protection** keeps your activity from escaping the encrypted tunnel.
- **Encrypted infrastructure** — our servers are hardened and access-controlled.

**Our no-logs promise, in plain language:**
- We do **not** log your browsing history.
- We do **not** track the websites or services you connect to.
- We do **not** store your real IP address alongside your activity.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Ever.

We keep only the minimum information required to operate your account and process your subscription — and we tell you exactly what that is in our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

**Pull quote:**
> A VPN that logs your activity isn't protecting your privacy. It's collecting it. We don't.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

**a) Information you provide to us**
- **Account information:** your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
- **Payment information:** billing details processed through our payment provider. We do not store your full card details — these are handled securely by our PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor (Paystack).
- **Communications:** information you provide when you contact support, complete a form, or correspond with us.

**b) Information we collect automatically**
- **Device and connection data:** device type, operating system, app version, and aggregated, non-identifying diagnostic information used to keep the Service running.
- **Website usage data:** information collected through cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (see our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies)).

**c) What we deliberately do NOT collect through AfroVPN**
Consistent with our strict no-logs commitment, when you use AfroVPN we do **not** log, monitor, or store:
- your browsing history or the websites and services you visit;
- the content of your network traffic;
- your originating IP address linked to your activity;
- DNS queries tied to your identity.

We cannot disclose what we do not collect.

## 6. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share information only in these limited circumstances:
- **Service providers:** trusted third parties who help us operate our Services (such as payment processing by Paystack, cloud hosting, and customer support tools), bound by contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
- **Legal requirements:** where required by valid law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Dreamscape Systems, our users, or the public. Note that our no-logs architecture means we cannot produce activity data we do not hold.
- **Business transfers:** in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case we will notify you and ensure your data remains protected under terms consistent with this policy.

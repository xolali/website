## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules for using AfroVPN and our other services (the "Services") provided by Dreamscape Systems Ltd. ("Dreamscape Systems", "we", "us", or "our"). It exists to protect our users, our infrastructure, and the wider internet. It forms part of our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

We built AfroVPN to protect privacy and provide access to the open internet. That freedom comes with responsibility: you must use the Services lawfully and fairly.

---

## 2. Prohibited Activities

You must **not** use the Services to:

**Illegal activity**
- Engage in, promote, or facilitate any unlawful activity under applicable law.
- Distribute or access material that is illegal, including child sexual abuse material (which we report and act on without exception).

**Harm to others**
- Harass, threaten, defame, or stalk any person.
- Distribute malware, ransomware, or other malicious code.
- Send spam, conduct phishing, or carry out fraud or identity theft.

**Network and security abuse**
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to any system, account, network, or data.
- Conduct denial-of-service attacks, port scanning, or intrusion attempts.
- Interfere with, overload, or disrupt our infrastructure or other users' access.

**Intellectual property violations**
- Infringe the copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights of others.

**Service abuse**
- Resell or commercially redistribute the Services without our written authorization.
- Circumvent usage limits, security measures, or access controls.
- Use the Services in any way that violates our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

## 3. Privacy and Enforcement

Because of our strict no-logs policy, we do not monitor your activity, and we cannot see what you browse. However, where we receive a valid, lawful complaint or report of abuse — for example, relating to our infrastructure, fraud, or unlawful content — we will investigate and act within the limits of the data we hold.

Where a violation is established, we may:
- issue a warning;
- suspend or terminate your account, with or without notice;
- decline a refund where the violation warrants it;
- report unlawful activity to the relevant authorities.

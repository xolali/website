## 1. Our Commitment

At Dreamscape Systems Ltd. ("Dreamscape Systems", "we", "us", or "our"), we want you to be confident in your purchase. This Refund Policy explains when and how you can get a refund for AfroVPN subscriptions. It forms part of our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

---

## 2. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **7-day money-back guarantee** on new AfroVPN subscriptions. If you're not satisfied for any reason, you can request a full refund within **7 days** of your initial purchase — no complicated questions.

To qualify:
- Your refund request must be made within 7 days of your original purchase date.
- The guarantee applies to your **first** purchase of a subscription plan.

---

## 3. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple:
1. Email **support@dreamscapesystems.com** from the email address associated with your account.
2. Include your account email and, if available, your order or transaction reference.
3. Tell us briefly that you'd like a refund under the money-back guarantee.

We'll confirm receipt and process eligible requests promptly.

---

## 4. How Refunds Are Processed

- Approved refunds are issued to your **original payment method** via our payment processor (Paystack).
- Refunds are typically processed within *[5–10]* business days, though the time for funds to appear depends on your bank or payment provider.
- You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.

---

## 5. Renewals

The money-back guarantee applies to initial purchases, not to automatic renewals. To avoid being charged for a renewal you don't want:
- Turn off **automatic renewal** in your account settings at any time before your renewal date, or
- **Cancel** your subscription before it renews.

We send a reminder email before each renewal. If you believe you were charged for a renewal in error, contact us at support@dreamscapesystems.com and we'll review it fairly.

---

## 6. Exceptions

Refunds may not be available where:
- the 7-day guarantee window has passed (except where required by applicable consumer law);
- the request relates to a renewal rather than an initial purchase (see Section 5);
- the account has violated our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms) or [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/acceptable-use), including fraud or abuse;
- the purchase was made through a third-party platform (such as an app store) with its own refund process — in that case, please follow that platform's refund procedure.

---

## 7. Your Statutory Rights

This Refund Policy is in addition to, and does not limit, any rights you may have under applicable consumer protection law. Where mandatory local law provides you with greater rights, those rights apply.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used. We also use similar technologies such as pixels and local storage, which we refer to collectively as "cookies" in this policy.

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

**a) Strictly necessary cookies**
These are essential for the website to work — for example, enabling you to log in securely, maintain your session, and complete a checkout. The website cannot function properly without them, and they do not require consent.

**b) Functional cookies**
These remember your choices and preferences (such as language or region) to provide a more personalized experience.

**c) Performance & analytics cookies**
These help us understand how visitors use our website — which pages are visited and how the site performs — so we can improve it. The data is aggregated and used to enhance the experience.

**d) Marketing cookies**
Where applicable, these are used to measure the effectiveness of our marketing and, with your consent, to show you relevant information. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over cookies:
- **Cookie banner / preference center:** when you first visit, you can accept or reject non-essential cookies and change your choices at any time via our cookie settings.
- **Browser settings:** most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the website from working.
- **Opt-outs:** you can opt out of certain analytics and marketing cookies through our preference center or the relevant third party's tools.

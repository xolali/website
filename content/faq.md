## AfroVPN — General

**6. What is AfroVPN?**
AfroVPN is a virtual private network (VPN). It creates an encrypted connection between your device and the internet, keeping your online activity private, securing your data, and giving you unrestricted access to the open web from anywhere.

**7. How does a VPN work?**
When you connect to AfroVPN, your internet traffic is routed through an encrypted tunnel to one of our secure servers before reaching the wider internet. This hides your activity from your internet provider and others on your network, protects your data, and lets you access the internet as if you were in the server's location.

**8. Is using a VPN legal?**
Using a VPN is legal in most countries, including across most of Africa. VPNs are standard tools used by businesses and individuals worldwide to protect privacy and secure data. You are responsible for ensuring your use complies with the laws of your country and our [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/acceptable-use). AfroVPN should never be used for illegal activity.

**9. Which devices does AfroVPN work on?**
AfroVPN works on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux. A single subscription protects multiple devices, so you can stay secure across your phone, laptop, and tablet.

**10. How many devices can I use at once?**
Your subscription covers multiple simultaneous connections so you can protect all your everyday devices on one account. The exact number is shown on our [Pricing page](/pricing).

**11. Will AfroVPN slow down my internet?**
A small reduction in speed is normal with any VPN because your traffic is encrypted and routed through a secure server. AfroVPN is optimized for African networks and uses high-performance protocols to keep that impact minimal. For the best speeds, connect to a server geographically close to you.

**12. Do you offer a free trial or money-back guarantee?**
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If AfroVPN isn't right for you, request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase — no questions asked. See our [Refund Policy](/legal/refund-policy) for details.

**13. How do I get started with AfroVPN?**
Choose a plan on our [Pricing page](/pricing), create your account, and complete your secure payment. Then download the app for your device, log in, and tap connect. You'll be protected in minutes.

## Privacy & Security

**14. Does AfroVPN keep logs of my activity?**
No. AfroVPN operates a strict no-logs policy. We do not monitor, record, or store your browsing history, the websites you visit, or your real IP address alongside your activity. We keep only the minimum information needed to run your account and process payment, as described in our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

**15. What encryption does AfroVPN use?**
AfroVPN secures every connection with AES-256 encryption — the same standard trusted by banks and governments worldwide. We use modern, secure VPN protocols including WireGuard® and OpenVPN.

**16. What is a kill switch, and does AfroVPN have one?**
A kill switch protects you if your VPN connection unexpectedly drops. AfroVPN's kill switch instantly blocks your internet traffic the moment the connection is interrupted, ensuring your real IP address and data are never exposed. Yes, AfroVPN includes one.

**17. Can AfroVPN see what I do online?**
No. Because of our no-logs architecture, we do not record or retain your online activity. Even if we were compelled to share data, there would be no browsing history to provide, because we never store it.

**18. Does AfroVPN protect me on public Wi-Fi?**
Yes. Public Wi-Fi at cafés, airports, and hotels is a common target for attackers. AfroVPN encrypts your entire connection, so your passwords, messages, and financial details stay protected even on untrusted networks.

**19. Will a VPN protect me from viruses and malware?**
A VPN protects your connection and privacy, but it is not a replacement for antivirus software. AfroVPN secures your data in transit; you should still use good security practices and reputable antivirus tools to protect your device.

**20. Do you sell my data?**
Never. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing. Our business model is your subscription — not your data. See our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).
